Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about mastering the fretboard.

Why is fretboard memorization so important?

Fretboard memorization bridges the gap between music theory and practical playing. Knowing notes instantly allows you to build chords, understand scales, and improvise freely without guessing. It's the foundation for creativity and confidence.

How does "Guess the Note" mode work?

This mode uses active recall. The tool highlights a random note, and you must identify it. This strengthens neural pathways, making learning faster than passive review.

How to use "Playing Practice" mode?

Start slow with the metronome. See a note, then find and play it on your guitar before the next beat. Enable the microphone for real-time feedback to connect theory with muscle memory.

Is FretMemo free?

Yes, 100% free. It's built to be a high-quality resource for guitarists worldwide.
